Introduction

OpenDataPolicies.org provides the open-data policies for cities and states all over the United States on one website. This site is operated on a non-profit, non-governmental, non-partisan basis. It was developed by the Sunlight Foundation with support from the What Works Cities project of Bloomberg Philanthropies.

We are continuously improving this site. Check out this project on GitHub and feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request!

Data sources

The information that makes up OpenDataPolicies.org comes entirely from public sources. Throughout the site, links are provided to original data sources, whenever possible.
